Lesson 03

For this lesson lets learn how to position your fingers in the upper keys of the keyboard.

Keyboard Keys Finger Position
R & T Left hand's index finger
E Left hand's middle finger
W Left hand's ring finger
Q Left hand's little finger
U & Y Right hand's index finger
I Right hand's middle finger
O Right hand's ring finger
P Right hand's little finger

  • For the space use the thumb.
  • While typing the letters's use little fingers to press shift.
    Ex. if your typing captial "T", "R", "E", "W", "Q" letters use right hand's little Finger to press shift key and for captical "Y", "U", "I", "O", "P" use Left hand's little finger to press shift key.
  • Important - While press the upper keys lift only that finger which key you want to type and don't lift other fingers.
    Ex. If you want to type "Y" lift only the right hand's index while keeping other fingers on the middle key of the keyboard.
