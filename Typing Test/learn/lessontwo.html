Lesson 02

For this lesson lets learn how to position your fingers in the middle keys of the keyboard.

Keyboard Keys Finger Position
F & G Left hand's index finger
D Left hand's middle finger
S Left hand's ring finger
A Left hand's little finger
J & H Right hand's index finger
K Right hand's middle finger
L Right hand's ring finger
; Right hand's little finger

  • For the space use the thumb.
  • While typing the letters's use little fingers to press shift.
    Ex. if your typing captial "G", "F", "D", "S", "A" letters use right hand's little Finger to press shift key and for captical "H", "J", "K", "L", ":" use Left hand's little finger to press shift key.
