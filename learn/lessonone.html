Lesson 01

Before going to start typing anything you should always keep your mind something.

  • First - Always sit straight.
  • Second - Keep your eight fingers on the middle keys of the keyboard and thumb on the spacebar key (as shown the image down below).
  • Third - Always look what your typing (look in the screen), never look into the keyboard.
